Our Mission

At Micro-action, we believe it is the little things that make all the difference. Micro-Action is a non-profit organization dedicated to making a difference. We believe people are more likely to voluteer their time in short intervals, and will do so if given the opportunity. Micro-Action is dedicated to providing flexible, commitment free ways that you can help your community and make a difference in whatever way you can.
